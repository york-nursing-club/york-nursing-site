The York College Nursing Club built this site to centralize the forms, policies, admission info, and clinical site details that students actually get stuck on. It's put together by your Cohort Secretary and Club leadership — not by the Department of Nursing — so treat it as a starting point, and always confirm anything deadline- or fee-related against the official source linked on each page.
