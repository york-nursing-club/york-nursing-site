Student Nurse Externships

Paid summer positions (usually 8–10 weeks) where nursing students work alongside an RN preceptor doing real patient care under supervision. They're one of the best resume-builders you can get before graduating, and several of these systems hire a good chunk of their externs back as new-grad RNs. Not run by York or the Club — you apply directly to each hospital system.

These links go stale — that's normal, not a site bug Externship postings open and close on their own calendar (most post job listings between January and April for a summer start) and hospital systems redesign their career sites constantly. If a link below 404s or redirects somewhere generic, search "[system name] nurse extern" — the program almost always still exists even if the URL moved.
Typical eligibility pattern (varies by system): rising senior / final-year BSN student, at least one completed med-surg clinical semester, GPA around 3.0–3.5+, current BLS/CPR certification, and 1–2 clinical references. Confirm exact requirements on each system's own page — they're not identical.

Queens · already a clinical site

Jamaica Hospital Medical Center

9-week student nurse extern program at this Level I Trauma Center — assigned to a unit with a preceptor RN, plus observation time in the ED and Critical Care. Requires 2 completed clinical semesters in acute care. Run through MediSys Health Network's careers portal (also covers Flushing Hospital, below).

Manhattan VA · already a clinical site

VA-STEP (Veterans Affairs)

The VA Student Trainee Experience Program — a national federal program, not specific to one hospital, but the Manhattan VA Medical Center (already one of our clinical sites) participates. 10 weeks, ~400 paid hours, junior year completed, 3.0+ GPA, U.S. citizenship required. Each VA facility posts its own listing, usually via USAJOBS — search "VA-STEP Manhattan" or contact the facility directly.

Queens / Long Island

Northwell Health

Runs one of the largest Summer Nurse Extern programs in the region — an 8-week paid preceptorship across most of its hospitals, including LIJ Forest Hills. Apply through your school; Northwell coordinates with nursing programs directly.

Manhattan / Westchester

NewYork-Presbyterian (NYP)

Summer Nurse Externship for senior nursing students — positions are typically posted each March/April. NYP also lists Nursing Attendant and Nursing Companion roles open to students.

Nursing Student Opportunities
Manhattan

Mount Sinai Health System

Summer Student Nurse Internship — a 10-week paid program pairing each intern with an RN mentor, plus weekly professional-development sessions. Highly competitive (hundreds of applicants for ~150 spots system-wide).

Manhattan / Long Island

NYU Langone Health

Summer Student Nurse Extern program for rising seniors with a med-surg clinical semester completed. Postings usually go up in January for a June start.

Manhattan · Oncology

Memorial Sloan Kettering (MSK)

Clinical Assistant Program (CAP) — 10 weeks full-time for senior-level students, rotating through inpatient, outpatient, and perioperative units. About 30 students accepted per year, so it's competitive.
