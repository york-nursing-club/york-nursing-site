FAQ

Pulled from the questions that actually repeat in the cohort chats every semester — not hypothetical ones. Names and identifying details are stripped; these are patterns, not quotes.

How does the Prep U / ATI adaptive quiz mastery system actually work?

Short version: the mastery level you need isn't a fixed, program-wide number — it varies by course and instructor. If your progress bar "won't move," it's usually because the required level for that specific course is higher than you expect, or the assigned chapter range extends past what's listed in the syllabus. See the full explanation on Tools & Platforms, and confirm the actual required level with your instructor rather than assuming it matches another course.

Where do I find my Kaplan access link?

It comes through a school email — easy to miss if you're not checking regularly. If you never got it, contact your instructor instead of assuming Kaplan doesn't apply to your course. It's free (covered by a school grant), so you should never need to pay for it.

Where's the DocuCare access code?

It's instructor-specific and posted on Brightspace for your lab section — not a single code shared across the whole program. Make sure you're using the code for your actual section, not one a classmate in a different section gives you.

I bought ShadowHealth directly instead of the free trial — where's my Tina Jones assignment?

This is a known gap — students who buy ShadowHealth access directly sometimes don't get the same assignments (like the Tina Jones case, HEENT assessment) that free-trial users see automatically. Contact your instructor to get it added rather than assuming you did something wrong.

What is CircleIn and why do I have it?

It's not being used as an academic study tool here, even though that's what it's built for generally. The Nursing Club uses CircleIn specifically to keep a membership roster — that's the only reason you have it. Don't go looking for course content there.

Is a clinical skills "demo + practice" session graded, or just practice?

This one doesn't have a confirmed answer yet — it's come up in cohort chat without a clear resolution. Confirm directly with your course instructor whether a given demo + practice session counts toward your grade. If you know the answer for a specific course, let a Club officer know so this can be updated with something more useful than "ask your instructor."

Where do I check if training videos (e.g. PPE, hand hygiene) have been posted yet?

Brightspace, under your specific course — not a school-wide announcement. These get posted on a course-by-course basis, so check the course you're actually enrolled in rather than waiting for a general notification.

The drug test rule confused me — why didn't the acceptance letter mention it clearly?

You're not alone — this is one of the most common costly mistakes new students make. The rule (no earlier than 2 weeks before classes start, but at least 1 day before) is often only clarified verbally at orientation, not clearly spelled out in the acceptance letter itself. Several students have tested too early and had to retake (and repay for) it. See the callout on Start Here — timing this correctly the first time saves you money.
