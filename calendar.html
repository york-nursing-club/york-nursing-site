Key Academic Dates

These are college-wide dates, not nursing-specific — but they govern the deadlines nursing students live by, especially the immunization compliance date below. Full grids are in source-docs/; this page just surfaces the ones that matter most.

These will need re-gathering each semester. Check whether the Registrar posts these at a predictable URL — if so, linking out directly is more reliable than manually updating this table every term.

The WA grade date is the one to circle. Miss your CastleBranch compliance deadline and you can be issued a WA for the semester regardless of your academic standing. See the full checklist on Start Here.
